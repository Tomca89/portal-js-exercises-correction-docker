Exercise: 
	
		Recently, we changed the color of one of our models. The model contained in the element with the ID: "model-1", is now colored: "red".

		The simple task here would be to getting the element, and changing it's color to red.

		Using Jquery, your task would be to trigger the CSS of the "model-1" and change the color approprietly.


Code: 


		$("#model-1").css("color", "red");
